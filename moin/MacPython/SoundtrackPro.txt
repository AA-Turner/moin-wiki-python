[http://www.68k.org/~michael/macosx/stpconvert.py stpconvert.py] uses ../AppscriptModule and [http://www.apple.com/finalcutstudio/soundtrackpro/ Soundtrack Pro] to convert a file to a different file format, sample format, or sample rate.

[http://www.68k.org/~michael/macosx/stpfindfix.py stpfindfix.py] uses ../AppscriptModule and [http://www.apple.com/finalcutstudio/soundtrackpro/ Soundtrack Pro] to detect and repair common audio defects such as clicks and pops, hum, and phase misalignment.
