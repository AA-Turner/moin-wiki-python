How to place a text file and delete the first 2 lines:
{{{
 story = doc.place(macpath, on=doc.pages[page], showing_options=False)
 story.lines[1:2].delete()
}}}

 rect = [76, 12, 284, 65]
 # left, top, height, width in default unit (e.g. mm)
