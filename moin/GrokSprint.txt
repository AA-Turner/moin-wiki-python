Grok Sprint

 * Where: Vilnius, Lithuania
 * When: after EuroPython, 12-14 july
 * Organizer: Martijn Faassen

We gather once again to work on [http://grok.zope.org Grok], a web framework based on Zope 3. Grok aims to make Zope 3 more accessible for beginners, and more agile for experienced developers. If you're interested in participating, please sign up here. For any questions, join the [http://mail.zope.org/mailman/listinfo/grok-dev grok-dev mailing list].
