=== General ===

  * [http://pygame.org Pygame.org] collects programming resources and modules for developing games with the Pygame module.

    * [http://pygame.org/shredwheat/solarwolf SolarWolf] an excellent action arcade game written with pygame.
    * [http://www.clickass.org/~tgz/pyddr/ pyDDR] a high quality dance game created with pygame.

=== Card Games ===

  * [http://www.oberhumer.com/opensource/pysol PySol] is an exciting collection of more than 200 solitaire card games.
