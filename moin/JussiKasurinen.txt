== Jussi Kasurinen ==

Email: jussi.kasurinen@lut.fi

LUT Home: [http://www.it.lut.fi/ihmiset/index_eng.html?uid=722 Entry]

An assistant with Lappeenranta University of Technology's Department of Information Technology, who is currently finalizing his master's thesis on adapting Python to introductory programming courses. 

Contributes to Python community by translating introductory material to finnish language and by writing finnish manuals on Imaging Library and Tkinter. Future prospects include updates for manuals to support Python 3.0 and hopefully some new material on networking and/or Python programming for handheld devices.
