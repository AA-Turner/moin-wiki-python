Several periodicals dedicated to the Python Programming language
exist (some active, some are archives).  Currently available:

* [http://pythonpapers.org/ The Python Papers] -- is new as of 2006, with free downloads of printable issues.  Please consider contributing an article to this volunteer-run publication!

* [http://www.pyzine.com/ PyZine] -- Published from 2002 to 2005, the archived articles are now available for viewing online without a subscription.

* [http://pythonjournal.cognizor.com/ Python Journal] -- Published around 2002, the archived articles are available online.
