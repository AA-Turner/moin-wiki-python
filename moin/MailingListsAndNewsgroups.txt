= Mailing Lists and Newsgroups =

Numerous mailing lists, forums, newsgroups exist covering Python usage in the general sense and in conjunction with specific topics and technologies. Here is a list of places to look for list/group information:

 * An official guide to [http://www.python.org/community/lists.html Python mailing lists and newsgroups].
 * A list of [http://mail.python.org/ mailing lists hosted at python.org] - note that some are more active than others.
 * The [wiki:CompLangPython comp.lang.python newsgroup].
