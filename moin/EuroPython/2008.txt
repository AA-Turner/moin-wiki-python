= EuroPython 2008 =

EuroPython 2008 will take place in Vilnius, Lithuania.

The ''centre of all things'' of EuroPython is the [http://mail.python.org/mailman/listinfo/europython mailing list].

== Meetings ==

Currently, a time is being decided for the second IRC meeting: see [http://doodle.ch/participation.html?pollId=nsgeye66vdy8uxtv this page] for details; add your name and availability to the list if you want to take part or just listen in. Details of the IRC meetings can be found here: ["/IRCMeetings"]
