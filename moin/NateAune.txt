Nate Aune is founder and president of [[http://www.jazkarta.com|Jazkarta, Inc.]] a Boston-based consulting and solutions provider specializing in Python-based web applications and Plone CMS implementations.

Nate has given talks at EuroPython, PyCon, PyConBrasil and many Plone conferences. He is also the organizer of the [[http://django.meetup.com/6/|Boston Django User Group]], [[http://python.meetup.com/181/|Boston Python User Group]] and the [[http://bostonplone.org|Boston Plone User Group]].

Nate blogs at http://nateaune.com and http://blog.jazkarta.com.
