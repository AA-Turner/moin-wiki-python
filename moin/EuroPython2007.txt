= EuroPython 2007 =

EuroPython 2007 will take place at the [http://www.revalhotels.com/en/Hotels/Lithuania/Reval-Hotel-Lietuva Reval Hotel Lietuva] in Vilnius, Lithuania from Monday 9th July to Wednesday 11th July.
