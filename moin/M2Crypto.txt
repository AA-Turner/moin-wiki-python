= M2Crypto sprint =
M2Crypto homepage: https://gitlab.com/m2crypto/m2crypto/

M2Crypto is the most complete OpenSSL wrapper for Python 2 and 3. M2Crypto makes it relatively easy to add cryptographic support and security to your Python applications. Things like SSL/TLS (server and client), S/MIME, ...

 * [[https://gitlab.com/m2crypto/m2crypto/issues|All open M2Crypto bugs]]
