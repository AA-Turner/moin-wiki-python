== Reporting Bugs for Python.org ==
oh oh oh, oh oh oh, oh oh oh ohhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhhh
If you wish to report a bug or to suggest an enhancement for the python.org site, please check first to see if a ticket already exists for the subject you are reporting:

http://pydotorg.python.org/pydotorg/report/1

If your bug has not been reported, please register on Trac and add a ticket with suitable categories and priorities:

http://pydotorg.python.org/pydotorg/newticket
