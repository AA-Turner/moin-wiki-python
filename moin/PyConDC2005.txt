= Dates and times =

PyCon will be taking place March 23-25, 2005.

Sprints will be taking place starting the weekend before the conference, from March 19 to March 22.

= Location =

PyCon 2005 will be held at GWU's [http://cafritz.gwu.edu/cafritz.php?id=1929&site=cafritz Cafritz Conference Center] is located on the third floor of the Marvin Center ('''#30 on the GWU map below'''). The main entrance of The Marvin Center is located on 21st Street between H and I Streets.
