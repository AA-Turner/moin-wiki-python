= Tokyo Pythonistas =

A primarily English speaking group which seeks to bring together all those interested in networking in Tokyo with aim of learning more about the programming language Python. 

We hold bi-monthly meetups in the Tokyo Area in a to promote learning and knowledge exchange in a friendly environment. 

Japanese speakers are also welcome and we intend to promote friendly links and support to other Japanese speaking Python groups in Tokyo.

We are active on LinkedIn at http://linkd.in/tyo_pythonistas and also on Twitter [[http://twitter.com/#!/tyo_pythonistas|@tyo_pythonistas]]
