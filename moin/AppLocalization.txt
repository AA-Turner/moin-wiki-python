=== Internationalization and localization efforts for Chinese, Japanese and Korean environments on Python. ===

  * CJKCodecs -  The CJKCodecs is a unified unicode codec set for Chinese, Japanese and Korean encodings. It supports full features of unicode codec specification and PEP293 error callbacks on Python 2.3. [[http://cjkpython.i18n.org/]]
