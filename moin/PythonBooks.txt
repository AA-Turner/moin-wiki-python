There are a variety of books about Python.  Here's a guide to them:

  * IntroductoryBooks  (gentle overviews of the language)
  * ReferenceBooks (much information in a small space)
  * Specific applications:
     * GuiBooks
     * JythonBooks
     * WebProgrammingBooks
     * WindowsBooks
     *  XmlBooks
     * ZopeBooks
  * Books in languages other than English: GermanPythonBooks, JapanesePythonBooks, KoreanPythonBooks.
  * OutofPrintBooks
