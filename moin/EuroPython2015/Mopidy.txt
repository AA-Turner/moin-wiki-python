= Mopidy sprint at EuroPython 2015 =

[[https://www.mopidy.com/|Mopidy]] is an extensible music server written in Python.

Mopidy plays music from local disk, Spotify, SoundCloud, Google Play Music, and more. You edit the playlist from any phone, tablet, or computer using a range of MPD and web clients.

During the EuroPython2015 sprints in Bilbao, Spain, we're going to sprint on Mopidy.


= Details =

 * When: Saturday July 25 and Sunday July 26 2015, from 09:30 to 18:00 UTC+2

 * Where:
   * Room TBD, in Bizkaia Aretoa, Bilbao, Spain
   * #mopidy on irc.freenode.net


= Sprint goals =

 * Get new people quickly up and running with Mopidy development:
   * Development environment setup guide: https://docs.mopidy.com/en/latest/devenv/

 * Work on anything Mopidy related, including Mopidy extensions and clients:
   * Easy starter issues: https://github.com/mopidy/mopidy/labels/Easy%20starters
   * Sprint milestone: https://github.com/mopidy/mopidy/milestones/EuroPython%202015%20sprint
