== Common Ideas ==

This page lists ideas that have been discussed frequently or at great length in order
 * to help contributors of ideas to easily evaluate their ideas compared with similar past proposals,
 * to help readers of giant ideas threads by providing summaries of the most salient points, and
 * to help PEP writers who have decided to take the leap and propose one of these ideas by providing some of the text for the PEP.
