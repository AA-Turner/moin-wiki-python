== checklist for holding pycon India 2011 ==

These are some points to be taken into consideration. Please add to this.

 * free hosting by an engineering/other college
 * 3 a/c auditoriums with projectors and power outlets.
 * one room for speakers to gather - with power outlets, tables and chairs
 * one room for volunteers and organisers to gather and coordinate
 * one or more rooms for BOFs and sprints
 * at least one lab with sufficient machines for tutorials - more would be welcome
 * copious signs directing people to the various rooms
 * at least one senior staff member - preferably enthusiastic about python - from the college side
 * a good number of local volunteers - again preferably with some interest in python
 * scope for audience from the hosting college
 * availability of hostel accomodation for attending students
 * availability of guesthouse/guestrooms for out station speakers
 * transport arrangements
 * available rooms/auditoriums in fairly close proximity to each other
