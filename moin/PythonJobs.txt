/!\ If you want to submit individual job postings, you can do so on the [[https://www.python.org/jobs/|Python Job Board]].

== International Job Boards ==
 * [[https://www.python.org/jobs/|Python Job Board]] - maintained by volunteers of the PSF
 * [[https://pythonjobs.github.io/|pythonjobs.github.io]] - another volunteer run effort to build a Python jobs board
 * [[https://www.openstack.org/community/jobs/|OpenStack jobs]] - all OpenStack is written in Python and most OpenStack companies are recruiting
 * [[https://www.reddit.com/r/PythonJobs/|r/PythonJobs]] - subreddit with regular Python job postings
