PyCon 2006 will be held near Dallas, Texas.

The main conference web site is http://us.pycon.org.

The venue will be the Dallas-Addison Marriott Quorum 
([http://www.stayatmarriott.com/Pycon2006/ reserve a room] / [http://marriott.com/property/propertypage/DALQC Hotel's Website] / [http://maps.yahoo.com/maps_result?addr=14901+Dallas+Parkway&csz=Dallas%2C+Texas+75254&country=us Map to Hotel]) 

The Marriott Quorum hotel is located on the north side of Dallas, in the suburban [http://www.addisontexas.net/ Town of Addison].

= Accommodations =

Rooms will be available at the Marriott Quorum at conference rates of $79/night for single and double rooms, $89/night for triple and quadruple rooms.  Local taxes of 13% will be added onto this nightly rate.

To obtain the room rate, you must register using the hotel's PyCon event page at http://www.stayatmarriott.com/Pycon2006/ .

Looking for a roommate to share costs with?  Use the [http://us.pycon.org/Addison/RoomSplitting RoomSplitting page] to find one.
