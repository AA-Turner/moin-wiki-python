= EuroPython Improvements =

This page is a collection of links to pages about improving the EuroPython conference, notably how it is organised as well as the general experience.

  * EuroPythonRoles - organiser and participant roles in EuroPython

Please join the europython-improve mailing list [http://mail.python.org/mailman/listinfo/europython-improve (info)] for discussion of EuroPython improvements.

If you wish to volunteer to help EuroPython in some way, consider joining the europython-volunteer-announce list [http://mail.python.org/mailman/listinfo/europython-volunteer-announce (info)] to receive notifications on this topic.
