== AbqPython is the local Python user's group in Albuquerque, New Mexico. ==

This group is mainly organized as a [[http://www.meetup.com/AbqPython/ | MeetUp Group]]. We generally meet on the first Tuesday of the month, but look at [[http://www.meetup.com/AbqPython/ | the MeetUp page]] for specific announcements. 

Members are encouraged to get themselves into [[FrontPage#use | the editor's group of this Wiki]] and to create their own pages to serve as a home for their Python (and other notes) and to contribute to this page the pages in the tree under it.
